在使用本应用前，请你认真阅读并充分理解本隐私协议的全部内容。你开始使用本应用，即视为你已阅读、理解并同意本隐私协议。

  • 应用名称：藏宝图（TreasureMap）
  • 运营者：张国庆
  • 联系方式：joygqz@gmail.com

二、我们处理的信息类型

在你使用本应用时，本应用可能会处理以下信息：

1. 你主动提供或保存的信息

  • 宝箱、宝物名称
  • 备注信息
  • 藏宝路径与位置步骤
  • 你拍摄或选择的图片
  • 编码、二维码相关内容
  • 你设置的有效期与提醒信息
  • 你自定义的编码规则设置

2. 设备权限相关信息

为实现对应功能，本应用可能会在获得你授权后访问以下系统能力：

  • 相机权限：用于拍摄物品照片、扫描二维码
  • 相册读取权限：用于从系统相册选择图片
  • 相册写入权限：用于将二维码图片保存到系统相册
  • 通知权限：用于发送有效期提醒
  • iCloud / CloudKit 能力：用于在你的 Apple 设备之间同步宝藏记录

3. 购买相关信息

当你购买或恢复“Pro”权益时，本应用会通过 Apple 提供的 StoreKit 服务读取商品信息、校验购买状态并恢复历史购买记录。

本应用不会直接接触你的银行卡号、支付密码或完整支付账户信息。相关支付、结算与验证流程由 Apple 负责处理。

三、信息的使用目的

本应用处理上述信息，仅用于实现以下目的：

  • 保存和管理你的宝箱、宝物及相关记录
  • 展示你上传或拍摄的图片
  • 生成、展示、保存和扫描二维码
  • 创建有效期提醒并发送本地通知
  • 在你已启用 iCloud 的情况下同步数据
  • 提供 Pro 权益购买、校验与恢复购买功能
  • 保障应用基础运行、处理异常并改进使用体验

四、信息存储与处理方式

1. 本地存储

你在本应用中创建的大部分内容默认存储在你的设备本地，包括但不限于宝箱与宝物记录、图片与路径步骤、编码设置以及提醒配置。

2. iCloud 同步

本应用启用了 Apple CloudKit 同步能力。在以下情况下，你的数据可能通过 Apple 的 iCloud 服务在你的设备之间同步：

  • 你的设备已登录 Apple 账户
  • 你的系统和账号环境支持 iCloud
  • 你未关闭相关 iCloud 能力

通过 iCloud / CloudKit 同步的数据由 Apple 提供基础设施支持，相关服务处理同时受 Apple 隐私政策及 Apple 服务条款约束。

3. 图片本地处理

本应用中的图片处理能力（例如前景主体提取）主要在设备本地完成。根据当前版本实现，我们未发现将你选取的图片主动上传至自建服务器进行识别处理的逻辑。

五、信息共享、转让与披露

除以下情形外，我们不会主动向第三方出售、出租、转让或共享你的个人信息：

  • 为实现 iCloud / CloudKit 同步，需要通过 Apple 提供的云服务处理相关数据
  • 为实现 App 内购买，需要通过 Apple 提供的支付与交易校验服务处理相关交易信息
  • 依据法律法规、司法机关或行政机关要求提供时
  • 为保护用户、运营者或社会公众的人身财产安全所必需时

根据当前版本实现，本应用未集成第三方广告 SDK，未发现第三方统计分析 SDK，也未发现将你的内容上传至自建业务服务器的逻辑。

六、权限使用说明

1. 相机权限

用于拍摄物品照片和扫描二维码。不授权不会影响你浏览已有内容，但相关拍照或扫码功能将无法使用。

2. 相册权限

用于从系统相册选择图片，或将二维码图片保存到系统相册。不授权时，对应图片导入或保存功能将无法使用。

3. 通知权限

用于在宝物即将到期或到期当天向你发送提醒。本应用仅发送与有效期相关的本地通知，不用于营销推送。

4. iCloud / CloudKit

用于在你的 Apple 设备之间同步你在本应用内保存的记录。若你不希望同步，可在系统设置中关闭相关 iCloud 权限或停用对应能力。

七、数据保留与删除

  • 你可以在本应用内修改或删除已保存的宝箱、宝物、图片、路径步骤等内容
  • 删除后，相关数据会从本地存储中移除；如该数据已同步至 iCloud，云端数据的删除和同步生效时间可能受 Apple 同步机制影响
  • 如果你卸载本应用，本地数据通常会随应用一并从设备中移除；但已经同步至 iCloud 的内容，可能仍保留在你的 iCloud 环境中，直到被同步删除或由你在 Apple 体系内清理

八、未成年人保护

如果你是未成年人，请在监护人阅读并同意本隐私协议后再使用本应用。监护人应对未成年人使用本应用及提交信息的行为进行指导和管理。

九、隐私协议的更新

我们可能根据产品功能、法律法规或运营需要对本隐私协议进行更新。更新后的版本将在本应用相关页面、发布页面或其他合理位置进行展示。若更新涉及你的重大权益，我们将尽量以显著方式提示。

TreasureMap Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-04-09

Welcome to TreasureMap (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we process relevant information, request system permissions, and store, use, and protect information when you use the App.

Please read and fully understand this Privacy Policy before using the App. By starting to use the App, you are deemed to have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

1. Operator Information

  • App Name: TreasureMap
  • Operator: Guoqing Zhang
  • Contact: joygqz@gmail.com

2. Types of Information We Process

When you use the App, the App may process the following information:

1. Information you actively provide or save

  • Names of treasure chests and treasures
  • Notes
  • Treasure paths and location steps
  • Photos you take or select
  • Codes and QR code related content
  • Expiration dates and reminder information you set
  • Your custom code rule settings

2. Device permission related information

To provide corresponding features, the App may access the following system capabilities after obtaining your authorization:

  • Camera permission: used to take photos of items and scan QR codes
  • Photo library read permission: used to select images from the system photo library
  • Photo library write permission: used to save QR code images to the system photo library
  • Notification permission: used to send expiration reminders
  • iCloud / CloudKit capability: used to sync treasure records across your Apple devices

3. Purchase related information

When you purchase or restore Pro benefits, the App uses StoreKit services provided by Apple to read product information, verify purchase status, and restore historical purchase records.

The App does not directly access your bank card number, payment password, or complete payment account information. The related payment, settlement, and verification processes are handled by Apple.

3. Purposes of Use

The App processes the information above only for the following purposes:

  • To save and manage your treasure chests, treasures, and related records
  • To display images you upload or capture
  • To generate, display, save, and scan QR codes
  • To create expiration reminders and send local notifications
  • To sync data when you have enabled iCloud
  • To provide Pro purchase, verification, and restore purchase features
  • To support the basic operation of the App, handle exceptions, and improve user experience

4. Information Storage and Processing

1. Local storage

Most content you create in the App is stored locally on your device by default, including but not limited to treasure chest and treasure records, images and path steps, code settings, and reminder configurations.

2. iCloud sync

The App has enabled Apple CloudKit synchronization capability. In the following circumstances, your data may be synchronized across your devices through Apple's iCloud service:

  • Your device is signed in to an Apple account
  • Your system and account environment support iCloud
  • You have not turned off the relevant iCloud capability

Data synchronized through iCloud / CloudKit is supported by Apple's infrastructure, and the related services are also subject to Apple's Privacy Policy and Apple service terms.

3. Local image processing

Image processing capabilities in the App, such as foreground subject extraction, are primarily completed locally on your device. Based on the current version implementation, we have not identified logic that actively uploads your selected images to self-hosted servers for recognition or processing.

5. Sharing, Transfer, and Disclosure of Information

Except in the following circumstances, we will not proactively sell, rent, transfer, or share your personal information with third parties:

  • To enable iCloud / CloudKit synchronization, relevant data needs to be processed through cloud services provided by Apple
  • To enable in-app purchases, relevant transaction information needs to be processed through payment and transaction verification services provided by Apple
  • When required by laws and regulations, judicial authorities, or administrative authorities
  • When necessary to protect the personal or property safety of users, the operator, or the public

Based on the current version implementation, the App does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs, no third-party analytics SDK has been identified, and no logic has been identified that uploads your content to self-hosted business servers.

6. Permission Usage Explanation

1. Camera permission

Used to take photos of items and scan QR codes. If you do not grant this permission, you can still browse existing content, but features related to taking photos or scanning codes will be unavailable.

2. Photo library permission

Used to select images from the system photo library or save QR code images to the system photo library. If you do not grant this permission, the corresponding image import or save features will be unavailable.

3. Notification permission

Used to send reminders when a treasure is about to expire or on the day it expires. The App only sends local notifications related to expiration reminders and does not use notifications for marketing.

4. iCloud / CloudKit

Used to synchronize records you save in the App across your Apple devices. If you do not want synchronization, you can turn off the relevant iCloud permission in system settings or disable the corresponding capability.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • You can modify or delete saved treasure chests, treasures, images, path steps, and other content within the App
  • After deletion, the relevant data will be removed from local storage. If the data has already been synchronized to iCloud, the deletion of cloud data and its synchronization effectiveness may be affected by Apple's sync mechanism
  • If you uninstall the App, local data will usually be removed from the device together with the App. However, content already synchronized to iCloud may still remain in your iCloud environment until it is deleted through synchronization or cleaned up by you within Apple's ecosystem

8. Protection of Minors

If you are a minor, please use the App only after your guardian has read and agreed to this Privacy Policy. Guardians should guide and manage minors in their use of the App and submission of information.

If an update materially affects your rights and interests, we will try to provide prominent notice.
